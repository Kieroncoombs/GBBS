Our Mission

At The Great British Bee Society, our mission is to protect and promote the well-being of bees and their vital role in our ecosystems. Bees are not just buzzing insects; they are incredible pollinators responsible for the reproduction of countless plant species, including many of the fruits, vegetables, and flowers we rely on for sustenance and beauty.

Together, with the support of our dedicated volunteers, donors, and partners, we are committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of bees and the ecosystems they sustain. Join us on this mission to protect and preserve these vital pollinators for generations to come.

What we've done

Created bee-friendly habitats

Our large team are planting native flowers, shrubs, and trees that provide bees with a diverse and abundant source of nectar and pollen. This helps sustain bee populations and supports their health.

Supported local beekeepers

We help with promoting local honey and supporting local beekeepers helps sustain their operations and promotes the health of honeybee populations.
